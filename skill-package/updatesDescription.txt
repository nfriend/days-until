Days Until has learned some new tricks!

You can now list all your countdowns by saying:

- "Alexa, ask Days Until to list all my countdowns."
- "Alexa, tell Days Until to show me all of them."

You can delete a countdown with:

- "Alexa, ask Days Until to delete a countdown."
- "Alexa, ask Days Until to erase my birthday."

You can receive daily reminders during the 10 days leading up to an event by saying:

- "Alexa, ask Days Until to create daily reminders."
- "Alexa, ask Days Until to set up reminders for my birthday."

Other changes include:

- Celebratory sound effects when checking a countdown
- Bug fixes
- More accurate date calculation
- Better event recognition
- More flexible conversation patterns

Days Until has also been updated to work great with video-enabled Alexa devices. Give it a try on your Echo Show or through your Amazon Fire TV Stick!
